## Implementation with MadHub

### TikTok Account Warm-Up

One of the key features of the **tk Account Health Tool** is the **TikTok Account Warm-Up**. This feature simulates actual user engagement by browsing content based on specific keywords. Here’s how it works:

1. **Configuration**: Set your interaction parameters — select your desired browsing mode (vertical nurturing for targeted content, or random nurturing for broader exposure).
2. **Operation**: This feature automatically likes and comments on posts, helping to establish your account's credibility and activity.

**Parameters to Set**:
- **Interaction Probability**: Customize how frequently you engage with content.
- **Execution Probability Distribution**: Control the randomness of interactions to mimic natural user behavior.

**Results**: Users will notice an increase in follower engagement, with a potential enhancement in visibility and reach within the TikTok community.

### TikTok User Search

Another valuable feature is the **TikTok User Search**, which allows you to **accurately target potential clients** by searching for users based on specific criteria.

1. **Input Parameters**: Specify your search criteria, which may include follower count, engagement metrics, and more.
2. **Automatic Interactions**: Once identified, you can automatically follow or send messages to these users.

**Benefits**:
- **Efficiency**: Rather than manually searching and engaging, automate the process to improve your lead generation efforts.
- **Higher Success Rate**: By targeting users who meet your specific criteria, you can expect to significantly improve your connection success rates.

**Configuration Steps**:
- Set filtering options based on follower count or engagement metrics.
- Use the input mode to directly specify your search parameters.
